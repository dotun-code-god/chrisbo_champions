Brief Overview of Chrisbo Club

Rise Your Hand to Help
Chrisbo Champions

Chrisbo Hb Champions Club is the brain child of Dr. Bose Ola born out of empathy for the duffering she witnessed from 1991 to 1996. Since when she was in charge of hematology day care at the RingRoad state hospital Ibdadan. She was also in charge of the sickle cell club of Adeoyo under the leadership of Dr. Titi Ipadeola assisted by Matron Odube and others. On retirement from the oyshmb in 2017, she decided to actualize this vision which she had registered with CAC since 2014 and couldn't do much because she juggling government service with private practice.
Our vision is to improve the quality of life of people living with sickle cell anemia by providing qualitative medical care and through education with seminars, lecturers and recent updates in sickle cell research.
